<ol> 
</ol>

<p><strong>示例 1：</strong></p>

<pre>
<strong>输入：</strong>nums = [5,2,3,1]

</pre>

<pre>
<strong>输入：</strong>nums = [5,1,1,2,0,0]
<strong>输出：</strong>[0,0,1,1,2,5]
</pre>
